Men's Ministry

Sharpening one another to be the men God has called us to be.

Our Mission

Our Men's Ministry exists to equip and empower men to be spiritual leaders in their homes, in the church, and in their workplaces. We challenge men to grow in their relationship with God and to build strong, accountable relationships with other men.
